WasteWarrior

WasteWarrior is an app designed to help people reduce household food waste through awareness, visibility, and better everyday habits.

  • Track food inventory and expiry
  • Understand waste patterns over time
  • Encourage more sustainable choices

When launched, a portion of premium features will support food banks and food-rescue organisations.
